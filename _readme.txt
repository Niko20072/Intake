CONTEXT:
You play as a witch named Vera, who loves farming and potion-making. One day, her magical broom is stolen. To get it back, Vera must earn 2000 coins and complete the mysterious payment left behind.
Plan your farming carefully, manage your resources, and choose your potions wisely.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO PLAY:
Grow magical plants, water them, and harvest them when ready.
Use the ingredients to craft potions with unique effects and complete orders to earn coins.
Manage your time and resources carefully as orders reset periodically.
Keep progressing until you earn 2000 coins and recover your stolen broom.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO WIN: Earn 2000 coins and complete the payment from the nightstand to recover your broom.
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO MAKE MONEY: complete orders
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CONTROLS:
-W, A, S, D - Move
-Left Click - Interact with objects
-E - Open inventory (You can switch between pages using the icons located at the bottom of the inventory: plants, potions, seeds)
-Q - Quit any interface (or move if you have the car or seeds interface open)
-R - Equip watering can
-X - Skip tutorial / cutscenes
-G - God mode (for debbuging)

!IMPORTANT!: You must be close to objects to interact with them (if you want to see the player range, uncomment the line 93 in the player.cpp file)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INTERACTABLE OBJECTS:
Outside:
-House door
-Car
-Farm tiles

Inside the house:
-Bed
-Table
-Nightstand

ADDITIONAL FEATURES:

HOW TO PLAY (read if you don’t get the tutorial):

-Press Start :) (you can adjust the music with the - / + keys)
-Press E to open your inventory. You can switch between frames using the buttons at the bottom of the screen (plant, potion, seed).
-Press Q/E to exit the inventory.
-Go to the car and left click on it (you need to be close to interact).
   You will see the seed shop. Click on the brown buttons with prices to buy seeds.
   You can see your money in the top left corner.
-Buy some seeds.
-Click on the envelope button in the car interface to see the orders.
   Orders are tasks you complete to earn money.
   You need to brew the required potions to send an order.
   You can’t complete an order unless you have all the required potions.
   Orders reset every 5 days, so be quick.
-Go to the field (tiles will highlight with a brown border when you hover over them).
   Left click on a tile near you (you must be close to it).
   A seed menu will appear. Press Plant to plant something.
-Press R to equip the watering can.
   You will see it at the bottom of the screen when selected (it will glow slightly).
   When equipped, left click on a plant or tile to water it.
-You need to water all plants before going to bed or they will die.
   Click on a plant to check if it’s ready or to collect it (the watering can must not be equipped while checking a plant).
-Click on the house door to go inside the house (again, you must be close to it).
-Inside the house you can interact with 3 objects: potion table, nightstand, bed
-Click on the potion table.
   Here you craft potions using your plants.
   Press Craft if you have enough ingredients.
   You can see your plants at the top of the screen.
   Switch between pages using the buttons at the bottom (1, 2, 3).
-Press Q to exit.
-Click on the nightstand.
   If you have enough money, press “Send 2000” to finish the game.
   (Press Q to exit)
-Click on the bed.
   Press Yes to sleep and go to the next day (plants will grow or die if not watered).
   Press No or Q to cancel.
-Press Q to exit the house.
-Repeat until you earn 2000 coins and finish the game.
